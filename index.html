Hello and Welcome

Friday is a lovable and playful dog who steals the hearts of everyone she meets. Her birthday on the 27th of
July is a day of celebration for her owners and friends alike. This furry friend has a unique palate, with a
particular fondness for vegetables, liver, and, of course, lots and lots of chicken. Her absolute favorite is KFC,
which she can never seem to get enough of!
Despite her love for food, Friday's enthusiasm for life is what truly sets her apart. Her wagging tail and
energetic personality bring joy to all those around her. Whether she is playing fetch in the park or cuddled up
on the couch, Friday is always happy and content. Her warm and affectionate nature is a constant reminder of
the love and happiness that our furry friends bring into our lives.
